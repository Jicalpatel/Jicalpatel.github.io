Fall 2024 Data Mining Recommendations

Co-Purchasing Analysis

A recommendation-focused analytics project that studies product relationships and co-purchase behavior to uncover useful buying patterns.

Project Story

The project focuses on identifying relationships between products that are frequently purchased together. This kind of analysis powers recommendation engines, cross-selling opportunities, inventory insights, and customer journey improvements.

For portfolio storytelling, this project shows the ability to move from raw transactional data toward clear business insight: clean the data, model relationships, identify patterns, and communicate what the patterns mean.

What makes it strong

  • Shows applied database and analytics thinking.
  • Connects technical analysis to business/product outcomes.
  • Uses recommendation-system language that recruiters recognize quickly.
